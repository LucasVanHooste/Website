BLB

A mobile game I released in July of 2019. It's available on the Google Play Store and features global leaderboards and advertisements.
I Developed the game in Unity, using the Google Playservices & Google Admob SDKs for the leaderboards and advertisements.



Gameplay

Tap the screen to jump! The goal is make it as high as possible without hitting any obstables or sliding off the screen. Once you lose, you restart all the way from zero.



Google Play

The game is available on Google Play so feel free to try it out.
