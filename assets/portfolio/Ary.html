Ary and the Secret of Seasons

Ary & the Secret of Seasons is a puzzle/action adventure game that is being developed Exiin (Brussels, BE), in cooperation with Fising Cactus (Mons, BE). I worked on the game for a period of four months, during my internship at Fishing Cactus. I was responsible for code optimization and gameplay polish.


Gameplay

The game lets you play as Ary, a young girl who journeys across the great world of Valdi in order to save it from disrupted seasons. By becoming the Guardian of Winter, you are able to manipulate the seasons around her. You can control the seasons and use them to defeat enemies, overcome obstacles, and solve complex puzzles on your adventure across Valdi.


Availability

The game is planned to be released in September 2020. It will be available on PC, PS4, XBOX One and Nintendo Switch.
